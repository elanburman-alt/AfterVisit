Yusuf,

Cora's introduction was generous, and your June gift to women and infants was generous too — different kinds of generosity, both noticed. Thank you for breakfast.

What you said about wanting to find a place that tells the truth about what works and what doesn't is the thing I'd most want any donor to be looking for. It's not a thing every program in this sector is set up to say honestly, and I won't pretend ours says it all the time, but it's the standard I want to hold us to in front of you.

Your cost-per-patient question deserves a real number rather than a marketing one. The unit cost for women-and-infants admissions sits in a band I can give you precisely at our next meeting; the more interesting question is the one you asked next — how donor dollars layer onto reimbursement — and that one is genuinely complicated. Dr. Naomi Ide, our medical director, can walk through it with you in a way I cannot.

If a second meeting in six weeks works, Dr. Ide and I can make ourselves available wherever's convenient for you. The breakfast spot was good; happy to come back there.
